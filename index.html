About us

The Faculty of Technology at Rajarata University of Sri Lanka

Faculty of Technology consists of five specialized departments Information and Communication Technology, Electrical and Electronic Technology, Material Technology, Bio Process Technology, and Food Technology. Each department focuses on innovative research and practical applications, preparing students for the evolving technological landscape.

Faculty of Technology Departments

ITT

Communication Technology
Information and

01

The Department of Information and Communication Technology focuses on developing cutting-edge solutions in software engineering, artificial intelligence, and data science. It equips students with strong programming skills and expertise in cybersecurity, ensuring they can tackle modern digital threats. The curriculum emphasizes cloud computing, networking, and big data analytics, preparing graduates for high-demand tech careers. Students gain hands-on experience through practical labs and industry collaborations, bridging the gap between theory and real-world applications. The department encourages research in machine learning, blockchain, and the Internet of Things (IoT) to drive technological advancements. Faculty members actively engage in innovative projects, fostering a learning environment that promotes creativity and problem-solving. The department collaborates with leading tech companies to offer internships and industry exposure. Graduates are well-prepared for roles in software development, IT consulting, and systems analysis. The department also supports startups and entrepreneurship, empowering students to create their own tech solutions. With a focus on innovation and industry relevance, it plays a key role in shaping the future of ICT in Sri Lanka.

EET

Technology
Electrical and Electronic

03

The Department of Electrical and Electronic Technology specializes in power systems, automation, and embedded systems. Students learn about electrical circuits, renewable energy technologies, and advanced control systems, gaining essential engineering skills. The department emphasizes hands-on training through well-equipped laboratories and industry-linked projects. Research areas include smart grids, power electronics, and IoT-enabled automation for industrial applications. Faculty members are dedicated to fostering innovation, guiding students in designing energy-efficient and sustainable solutions. Collaboration with industries ensures students gain real-world exposure, making them competitive in the job market. The department also focuses on robotics and embedded system design, allowing students to work on cutting-edge innovations. Graduates are well-equipped for careers in electrical engineering, automation, and industrial electronics. Entrepreneurship is encouraged, with opportunities for students to develop their own tech-based solutions. The department plays a crucial role in advancing electrical and electronic technologies for a sustainable future

MTT

Material Technology

03

The Department of Materials Technology focuses on the study, development, and application of advanced materials for various industries. It covers areas like nanotechnology, polymer science, and composite materials, enabling students to innovate in aerospace, construction, and automotive sectors. Research on sustainable materials, biodegradable plastics, and energy-efficient designs is a key aspect of the department. Students gain practical experience in material testing, characterization, and manufacturing processes. Faculty members engage in cutting-edge research, collaborating with industrial partners for technology transfer. The department encourages innovation in developing stronger, lighter, and more durable materials. Graduates find opportunities in research institutions, manufacturing companies, and material engineering fields. The program emphasizes sustainability, ensuring that students understand eco-friendly production methods. Industry partnerships provide students with internships and hands-on experience in material processing. This department plays a vital role in advancing materials science for modern technological needs.

BPT

Bio Process Technology

04

The Department of Bio Process Technology integrates biological sciences with engineering to develop sustainable solutions in biotechnology, agriculture, and environmental management. The curriculum covers bioenergy production, bioprocess optimization, and microbial technology for industrial applications. Students learn about fermentation technology, bioreactor design, and waste management strategies. Research focuses on eco-friendly processing methods and the use of biotechnology in food, pharmaceuticals, and environmental conservation. Practical training in bioprocessing labs ensures students gain hands-on experience in applying biological principles to industrial challenges. Faculty members collaborate with industries and research institutions to develop innovative bio-based solutions. Graduates can pursue careers in biomanufacturing, environmental engineering, and bio-product development. The department promotes entrepreneurship, encouraging students to develop bio-based startups. Emphasis is placed on sustainable development, reducing environmental impact through innovative bioprocessing methods. With a strong research and application focus, the department contributes significantly to the advancement of biotechnology and sustainable practices.

FDT

Food Technology

05

The Department of Food Technology focuses on the science and technology behind food production, processing, and safety. Students learn about food chemistry, microbiology, and quality control, ensuring the production of safe and nutritious food products. The curriculum includes food preservation techniques, packaging innovations, and regulatory compliance for food industries. Research areas include functional foods, nutraceuticals, and sustainable food processing methods. Practical training in food laboratories and industry collaborations provide students with hands-on experience. Faculty members engage in innovative research to develop healthier, longer-lasting, and more sustainable food products. Graduates can pursue careers in food manufacturing, quality assurance, and product development. The department emphasizes food security, addressing challenges like waste reduction and nutritional improvements. Industry partnerships offer students exposure to real-world food production environments. With a focus on innovation and sustainability, the department plays a crucial role in advancing food science and technology.
